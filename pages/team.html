Meet The Team

Gilhan Dandin

Chief Executive Officer

Alongside a degree in psychology at the university of Oslo, Gilhan has many years of experience in tech, starting as a data trainee working with improving Google Home speech recognition technology in Norway and later as a data research analyst at Vainu. Plagued by constant back pains due to long hours at the office, he and other team members founded HealthyChair - a company focused on improving health and wellness at the office desk.
